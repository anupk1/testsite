This 10-week cohort-based course is ALL you need to get your

Taught by consultants from top firms, you’ll learn everything from the fundamentals of strategy consulting followed by real-life problem solving assignments so you can dive deeper into the roles & responsibilities of a consultant.

WEEK 0
Induction
You will be able to understand the fundamentals of consulting, connect with fellow participants, complete an initial assessment, and also meet your peers across the city wide meetups we conduct
  • Onboarding Call
  • Networking Session
  • Initial Assessment
  • Prereads (Consulting Overview)
  • Offline Meet-ups across cities
week 1
Introduction to Consulting
Gain a thorough understanding of the consulting industry, its diverse career trajectories, necessary skills, and the inner workings of consulting firms. This knowledge will empower you to make well-formed decisions about your potential engagement in this field.
  • Understand the role, functions, and daily activities of consultants
  • Explore diverse career paths and strategies to overcome entry barriers
  • Gain insights into prominent consulting firms and their hiring processes
  • Identify key skills, opportunities, and recent industry trends
  • Consultant's Toolkit
  • Discover various consulting practice types and their workflow
  • Engage in a real-life case study and self assessment session
week 2
Business and Data 101
You will be able to understand the fundamentals of consulting, build a consultant's toolkit, perform various consultant tasks such as research, analysis, and production, make data-driven business decisions, and effectively communicate findings
  • The 3 core tasks - research, analysis, and production.
  • Research Deep Dive: Types of research, sources, and leveraging AI for research.
  • Analysis Deep Dive: Excel basics, data interpretation for consultants, introduction to supplementary tools : SQL and PowerBi.
  • Accelerating Analysis with AI: Introduction to Code Interpreter for data analysis.
  • Developing storytelling and presentation skills.
  • Consulting Project Types: Understanding different project archetypes.
  • Discuss real-life case studies
  • Project/Assignment
week 3
Key Consulting Concepts - Part 1
You will be adept at applying key consulting frameworks and strategies to solve complex business problems.
  • Introduce the 7-Step Problem Solving Framework from McKinsey
  • Discuss frameworks used at BCG and Bain
  • Explore MECE
  • Examine Issue Trees
  • Delve into Hypothesis Building
  • Utilize AI for case structuring assistance
  • Engage in a Project/Assignment
week 4
Key Consulting Concepts - Part 2
You will be able to apply essential concepts such as Pareto Principle and BCG Growth Matrix in real-world business scenarios, effectively manage projects using GANNT charts and RACI matrices, and leverage AI to enhance case structuring skills. You'll also gain practical experience through case studies and projects.
  • Explore Pareto Principle and its practical business applications.
  • Delve into BCG Growth Matrix and its relevance in business strategy.
  • Learn how to create GANNT charts for project management
  • Engage in comprehensive case studies and project work.
  • Utilize AI-powered prompts to enhance case structuring skills.
  • Complete a project/assignment.
week 5
Key Consulting Concepts - Part 3
You will be able to effectively apply key consulting concepts to real business situations, analyze cases, and utilize AI support for case structuring. Additionally, you'll have access to diverse frameworks and templates for addressing specific business challenges.
  • Explore Key Consulting Concepts: Top-down and bottom-up analysis, benchmarking, sensitivity analysis, scenario analysis, balanced scorecard, and pyramid principle.
  • Emphasize Business Applications
  • Dive into Case Studies
  • Leverage AI Support
  • Engage in Projects/Assignments.
week 6
Case Simulation Mastery
You'll be able to navigate live case simulations, analyze case studies proficiently, tackle the MBB Case Project independently, and excel in a two-day project for real-world challenges.
  • LIVE CASE SIMULATION [work in teams and come up with a business strategy]
  • Comprehensive case walkthrough
week 7
Consulting Career Preparation and Networking Mastery
You will be able to confidently create and enhance your professional documents, understand the consulting hiring process, and effectively utilize LinkedIn and cold emailing for networking and outreach in the consulting industry.
  • Identify key attributes consulting firms seek in candidates.
  • Consulting hiring process and its interview types.
  • Enhance your CV and cover letter.
  • Job application workflow.
  • Develop your personal narrative for job applications.
  • LinkedIn profile optimisation.
  • Master the art of cold emailing for outreach.
  • Engage in a project.
week 8 and 9
Case Interview Preparation
You'll be capable of foundational problem-solving and basic quantitative analysis using charts.
  • Guesstimates
  • Case Interview Introduction
  • Problem Understanding
  • Problem Structuring
  • Quantitative Analysis with Charts
  • Brainstorming Techniques
  • Case Closing Strategies
  • Types of cases with comprehensive examples and practice cases
  • Sample Interviews for Various Case Types
  • Project/Assignment
week 10
FIT Interview Preparation
Gain a thorough understanding of the consulting industry, its diverse career trajectories, necessary skills, and the inner workings of consulting firms. This knowledge will empower you to make well-formed decisions about your potential engagement in this field.
  • Understanding FIT interviews and interviewer expectations
  • Avoiding common pitfalls
  • Understanding Consulting Firm Culture
  • Mastering common questions and framing responses effectively
  • Navigating FIT interviews at MBB firms
  • Ethical Dilemmas and Case-Based Questions
  • Personal Experience and Storytelling
  • Putting it all together

FAQs: Here’s everything you may ask...

What happens after I apply for the program?
As soon as you press the ‘Apply Now’ button, you’ll be required to fill out the application form to apply for the ‘ Management Consulting ’ program.
‍
Our experts will then review each application to select only the ones who are aligned with our goal and values.
‍
Successful candidates will then receive an email with the next steps.
I am curious to build a career in Management consulting, but I’m not sure if it is the right career path for me. Should I apply for this program?
YES! In this program, you will get…

Introduced to the key concepts of consulting (so you can add a unique value to your current role by thinking in a more strategic rather than a tactical way)

LIVE simulation of an MBB project (so you know the exact roles & responsibilities of a consultant working with the big 3)
‍
GrowthSchool's  Management Consulting program will, in fact, help you make an informed career choice early on!
Is this a completely LIVE program?
No. This is a hybrid course with pre-recorded content and LIVE sessions. 
‍
Every week you will get access to on-demand content. And on the LIVE calls, you can get all your questions answered in the Q&A sessions.
How much time do I need to set aside for the program?
GrowthSchool Management Consulting program is a 10-week cohort setup that includes both pre-recorded course material and weekly LIVE sessions with the mentor and the guest speakers.

The LIVE sessions are approximately 90-mins long.

Apart from that, you’ll need to set aside 4-5 hours per week for 10-weeks as you’ll get projects to practically work on.
When do the LIVE calls & guest sessions happen?
The program has Weekly LIVE Zoom sessions with the program directors and other expert guest speakers across 10-weeks.

We recommend everyone to attend live calls, it’s energizing to see the entire group together. 

But program recordings and notes from the live sessions will be available within 48 hours for those who can’t attend live.
What if I can’t make it to the LIVE sessions?
Growthschool Management Consulting  Program is designed to work with your schedule.

We encourage you to attend all the live calls so you don’t miss out on the “breakout rooms”, where you get to collaborate with fellow marketers & discuss in small Zoom groups.

However, if you’re unable to make it LIVE, you will get access to the session recordings within 48 hours of the call.
How much access do I get to the mentors?
We can’t promise that they will reply to every single message, but they will be active during the live sessions.
‍
They will answer questions, provide feedback, and share their private notes to address common pain points.
What’s included with the program?
This program takes place online, so no travel is required.
‍
With your tuition, you’ll have one-year access to all the pre-study material, recordings of the live cohort sessions, guest sessions. You also get lifetime access to a private online community where you’ll interact with your batchmates.
Do I have lifetime access to the course material?
No. You will have access to the content for ONE YEAR.
What deliverables will I walk away with?
This program is all about taking action.
